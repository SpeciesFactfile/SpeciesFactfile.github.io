HUMPBACK WHALE

Humpback Whales engage in an activity called Breaching, where they leap straight out the water. Its not clear why they do this but its a pretty cool sight.

Humpback Whales migrate huge distances like to the Hawaiian islands to give birth to their young in warm tropical waters.

SPRINGBOK

Springboks engage in a activity called 'Pronking' where they bounce about across the plain. It's not clear why they do this but its thought they jump for joy!

They can leap as high as 2 meters!

Springboks are the national animal of South Africa and have the rugby team named after them ( THE BOKKE )!

GIRAFFES

All Giraffes have a prehensile tongue spanning 30cm meaning they can use them to clean their eyes!
